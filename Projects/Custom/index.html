About Anjana Beauty Care

Anjana Beauty Care was founded by Alka Bhalara three decades ago. Since its inception, Alka Bhalara stuck to her core values of Sewa and Excellence and made ABC more than a beauty parlour- a social movement where she intends to empower every possible stakeholder. Today ABC has earned a name for delivering high-quality output coupled with their remarkable ethics and empathy. With diverse products and services, ABC intends to bring excellent results at an affordable price.

Bridal

Marriage is no doubt, the biggest day in a life of a woman. Making sure, that you have that fab look, is of paramount important to us. Our bridal packages include eyebrow styling, threading, facial, full body waxing, manicure etc such that you look perfect on your big day!

Hair Treatment

Hair is what defines your personality. We, at Anjana Beauty Care make sure that your hair get the perfect treatment that will make you stand out. Be it, hair styling, hair color or hair cutting, we have got you covered! Our stylists understand the trends of today and will help you create the picture perfect look that you need.

Makeup

Sometimes, makeup is what you need to bring the best out of you. With our makeup services, we make sure that you look your best! We use skin friendly products so that no harm is done to your skin.
